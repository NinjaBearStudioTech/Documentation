### Hit Reactions

Once incoming damage is confirmed, a **hit reaction** can be triggered. The Damage Manager component will trigger the 
appropriate Damage Gameplay Event, which might activate the [Hit Reaction Ability](cbt_ability_hit_reaction.md), if granted to the owner.
