# Create Melee Attacks
<primary-label ref="combat"/>
<secondary-label ref="how-to"/>

## Goal

In this how-to, you will learn how to create your first **Attacks**, which can be used in standalone or as part of a
**Combo**.

## Pre-requisites

1. Your character is ready to receive new Abilities and play Animation Montages.
2. You have the Animation Sequences for your a **light** and a **heavy** attack (or similar).
